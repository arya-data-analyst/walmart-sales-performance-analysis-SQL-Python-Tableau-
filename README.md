# walmart-sales-performance-analysis-SQL-Python-Tableau-
End-to-end data analysis project on Walmart sales data, involving data cleaning and querying using SQL, data processing with Python, and visualization through an interactive Tableau dashboard. The project highlights key insights such as sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product trends using Excel, SQL, Python, and Tableau.

# 📊 Walmart Sales Performance Dashboard (SQL + Python + Tableau)

## 📝 Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end data analysis workflow on Walmart sales data. The objective is to analyze transactional data, extract meaningful business insights, and build an interactive Tableau dashboard to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Walmart lacks a centralized system to monitor sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior across branches, cities, and product categories. This project aims to solve that by building an interactive dashboard with actionable insights.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze sales and profit performance
* Identify top-performing branches and categories
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Track seasonal and hourly sales trends

---

## 🧰 Tools & Technologies Used

* **SQL** → Data querying
* **Python (Pandas, NumPy)** → Data cleaning
* **Excel** → Data preparation
* **Tableau** → Dashboard visualization

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* 💰 **Total Sales:** $1.21M
* 📈 **Total Profit:** $476.14K
* 🧾 **Average Order Value:** $121.35
* 📦 **Total Quantity Sold:** 23,483

👉 **Profit Margin ≈ 39.3%** (476K / 1.21M)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 🏬 Branch Performance

* Top Branch: WALM009 (~$25.7K)
* Sales variation across branches is relatively small (~20% difference)

👉 Insight: Sales are **evenly distributed**, but top branches outperform by ~15–20%

---

### 🌆 City Insights

* Sales concentrated in major urban areas
  👉 Insight: Opportunity to improve sales in **low-performing cities**

---

### 🛍️ Category Performance

#### 🔹 Sales Contribution

* Fashion Accessories: 489K (~40%)
* Home & Lifestyle: 489K (~40%)
* Remaining categories: ~20% combined

👉 Insight: **~80% of revenue comes from 2 categories**

---

#### 🔹 Best Selling Categories (Quantity)

* Fashion Accessories: 9,653 (~41%)
* Home & Lifestyle: 9,610 (~41%)
* Others combined: ~18%

👉 Insight: Strong **sales concentration in top 2 categories**

---

#### 🔹 Profit Insights

* Electronic Accessories generates highest profit (~30.7K)

👉 Insight: High sales ≠ High profit → **margin optimization needed**

---

### ⭐ Customer Ratings

* Fashion Accessories & Home → Highest ratings (~26K each)
* Other categories → Very low engagement (~1K–2.5K)

👉 Insight:

* High sales categories also have **high customer satisfaction**
* Low categories may have **quality or demand issues**

---

### 💳 Payment Method Distribution

* Credit Card: ~190K (~40%)
* E-wallet: ~185K (~38%)
* Cash: ~99K (~22%)

👉 Insight: **~78% transactions are digital**

---

### 📈 Monthly Sales Trend

* Peak Months:

  * December: $253K (~21%)
  * November: $245K (~20%)
* Lowest Months:

  * April–July: ~$25K–31K (~2–3% each)

👉 Insight:

* **~40% of total sales occur in last 2 months**
* Strong seasonal dependency

---

### ⏰ Hourly Sales Trend

* Peak Hour: 3 PM (~$142K)
* Strong hours: 3 PM – 7 PM (~60% of daily sales)
* Lowest: Late night (~0–15K)

👉 Insight:

* Sales peak in **afternoon & evening**
* Minimal activity after 9 PM

---

## 🔥 Strategic Insights

1. **Revenue Concentration Risk**

   * ~80% sales from 2 categories
     → Need diversification

2. **Seasonal Dependency**

   * ~40% sales in Nov–Dec
     → Heavy reliance on holiday season

3. **Digital Payment Dominance**

   * ~78% transactions are cashless
     → Promote digital offers

4. **Peak Time Optimization**

   * ~60% sales between 3 PM – 7 PM
     → Optimize staffing & promotions

5. **Underperforming Categories**

   * Contribute <20% of total sales
     → Need marketing & pricing strategies
